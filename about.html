Who is Kayesu?

Hi! I’m Allen Kayesu.
I wear many hats—I’m a daughter, a sister, an aunt, a niece, a cousin, and a friend (basically, the whole family tree wrapped into one). Born and raised in Rwanda, I’m currently pursuing a degree in Computer Science at New York University Abu Dhabi. My favorite thing to do is spending time with my family and friends. Maybe a little weird; I have an impressive knowledge of Cocomelon songs—not exactly by choice (my nephews could give you a very detailed explanation for this). I love meaningful conversations, going for walks and the little moments that make life special.
